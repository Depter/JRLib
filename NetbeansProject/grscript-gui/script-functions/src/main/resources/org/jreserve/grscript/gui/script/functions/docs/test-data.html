Test Data

There are some datasets readily avaiable to try out some other functions.

  • apcPaid(): paid incremental triangle from the APC dataset.
  • apcPaid(): incurred incremental triangle from the APC dataset.
  • apcNoC(): incremental claim numbers from the APC data set.
  • apcExposure(): burning costs from the APC dataset.
  • mclPaid(): paid incremental triangle from "Bootstrap Estimation of the Predictive Distributions of Reserves Using Paid and Incurred Claims", by Huijuan Liu and Richard Verrall.
  • mclIncurred(): incurred incremental triangle from "Bootstrap Estimation of the Predictive Distributions of Reserves Using Paid and Incurred Claims", by Huijuan Liu and Richard Verrall.

Examples

data = mclPaid()
printData "MCL-Paid", data

exposure = apcExposure()
printData "Exposure", exposure
